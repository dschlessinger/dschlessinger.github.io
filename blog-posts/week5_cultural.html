Cultural Blog: Week 5

Pairing

August 15, 2014

"Ok, just go ahead and type in that H1 heading on Line 35," I said.

"I already did," he said. "I already finished the body text. You're not seeing it?"

"No, and now I can't see your video either...let's just take a break."

Pairing. Like any relationship, communication is everything. Even when you and your pair are on the same page, the barrier of a computer screen, a few hundred miles, and a malfunctioning Stypi page can ruin everything. That said, pair programming works in the absence of logistical challenges.
