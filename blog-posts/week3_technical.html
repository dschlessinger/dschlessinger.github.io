The enduring popularity of JavaScript in web development

August 2, 2014


Week 3 was a brief introduction to JavaScript. Although I feel like I understand the concepts in this section, I am not very confident about how to integrate them with my working knowledge of HTML and CSS.   • #1: JavaScript started on Netscape Navigator. Hopefully, you never had to experience this terrible browser, but it really was one of the first of its kind. As a result, JavaScript gained a stronghold as a browser-oriented programming language from early on in the development of the web.
