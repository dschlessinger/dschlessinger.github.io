Technical Blog: Week 2

Margins vs. Borders vs. Padding

July 27, 2014


Borders:

  • This is the outline around a piece of content.
  • Analogy: Your bed frame.

Padding:

  • This is the space in between the content and the border.
  • Analogy: The empty space on your bed that your body naturally doesn't fill up.

Margins:

  • This is the space in between a border and another element.
  • Analogy: The space in between you and your roommate's beds.

See, isn't that way better than this?
