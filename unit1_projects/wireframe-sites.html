Wireframe Breakdown: NYTimes.com

July 22, 2014

The New York Times has to organize a huge amount of information for any reader. That means that their site must be incredibly easy to follow. It makes sense, then, that their site is arranged in columns, which help the eye follow the numerous stories they have on the page.

One thing I think the NYT site could improve is perhaps adding a few more photos or spacing stories apart a bit more. Since there is so much text, it can be easy to get lost in the page. I am used to their site so much now that I don't even think about it - but I can definitely understand how others might find it slightly difficult to follow.
